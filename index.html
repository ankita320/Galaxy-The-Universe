~Galaxify The Universe!~

You have been set out on a mission to save the new universe that has been discovered by planet Earth. Your job is to do your part in saving this new, foreign universe. To do this, you need to be sorted into one of the galaxies, then find your superpower and your animal companion. You will use your new sources of power to save and protect the new universe. Can you do it? There is only one way to find out. Get... Started!
